# Why Roadmaps Fail

Traditional roadmaps treat product development as a series of predetermined steps. This model breaks down when reality introduces uncertainty.

## The Core Tension

Roadmaps serve two masters poorly:
- Stakeholders want predictability
- Teams need flexibility

Attempting to satisfy both often results in a document that becomes obsolete the moment it's published.

## A Different Frame

What if we stopped asking "what will we build?" and started asking "what problems are we trying to solve?" The former locks in solutions prematurely. The latter creates space for learning.

This reframe is gaining traction, but implementation remains challenging. More patterns emerging from teams that have made the shift.
